# Style Presets for Slide Maestro

Curated visual styles. Each preset defines: vibe, best for, fonts, palette, signature elements.

## 1. Bold Signal
- Vibe: confident, high-impact, keynote-ready
- Best for: pitch decks, launches, statements
- Fonts: Archivo Black + Space Grotesk
- Palette: charcoal base (#1a1a2e), hot orange focal (#ff6b35), crisp white text
- Signature: oversized section numbers, high-contrast card on dark field

## 2. Electric Studio
- Vibe: clean, bold, agency-polished
- Best for: client presentations, strategic reviews
- Fonts: Manrope (variable weight)
- Palette: black (#000), white (#fff), saturated cobalt accent (#2563eb)
- Signature: generous whitespace, single accent color, precise grid

## 3. Dark Botanical
- Vibe: sophisticated, organic, premium
- Best for: brand presentations, luxury products, wellness
- Fonts: Cormorant Garamond + Montserrat Light
- Palette: deep forest (#0d1b0e), warm cream (#f5f0e8), gold accent (#c9a84c)
- Signature: subtle leaf/botanical patterns, serif headlines, warm tones

## 4. Creative Voltage
- Vibe: energetic, creative, attention-grabbing
- Best for: creative pitches, marketing campaigns, youth brands
- Fonts: Clash Display + General Sans
- Palette: electric violet (#7c3aed), neon lime (#84cc16), dark navy (#0f172a)
- Signature: diagonal splits, bold color blocks, dynamic angles

## 5. Neon Cyber
- Vibe: futuristic, tech, cutting-edge
- Best for: tech launches, AI/ML presentations, developer conferences
- Fonts: JetBrains Mono (headings) + DM Sans
- Palette: deep black (#0a0a0a), cyan glow (#06b6d4), magenta pulse (#ec4899)
- Signature: glow effects, grid overlays, monospace headings

## 6. Swiss Modern
- Vibe: clean, precise, trustworthy
- Best for: corporate reports, data presentations, consulting
- Fonts: Inter (justified exception for Swiss style) or Helvetica Neue + Source Serif Pro
- Palette: white base, charcoal text (#1f2937), red accent (#dc2626)
- Signature: grid-based, lots of whitespace, minimal decoration, strong hierarchy

## 7. Notebook Tabs
- Vibe: approachable, workshop-like, hands-on
- Best for: workshops, tutorials, team meetings
- Fonts: Bricolage Grotesque + Plus Jakarta Sans
- Palette: warm white (#faf9f6), pencil gray (#6b7280), highlighter yellow (#fbbf24)
- Signature: hand-drawn accents, tab-like navigation, notebook feel

## 8. Paper and Ink
- Vibe: editorial, literary, thoughtful
- Best for: thought leadership, publishing, academic
- Fonts: Playfair Display + Source Serif Pro
- Palette: cream paper (#fdf6e3), deep ink (#1a1a1a), burgundy accent (#7f1d1d)
- Signature: large pull quotes, generous margins, serif-heavy

## 9. Split Pastel
- Vibe: fresh, modern, approachable
- Best for: startup pitches, product demos, SaaS
- Fonts: Syne + Plus Jakarta Sans
- Palette: soft lavender (#e9d5ff), mint (#bbf7d0), peach (#fed7aa), white
- Signature: split-screen layouts, pastel color blocks, rounded cards

## 10. Vintage Editorial
- Vibe: classic, authoritative, timeless
- Best for: annual reports, heritage brands, institutional
- Fonts: Fraunces + Lora
- Palette: aged paper (#f4efe1), dark walnut (#3d2b1f), copper accent (#b87333)
- Signature: decorative borders, editorial grid, vintage texture

## 11. Pastel Geometry
- Vibe: gentle, structured, modern
- Best for: education, health, non-profit
- Fonts: Outfit + DM Sans
- Palette: soft blue (#bfdbfe), pale rose (#fce7f3), light sage (#d1fae5), charcoal text
- Signature: geometric shapes as decoration, soft shadows, clean lines

## 12. Monochrome Gold
- Vibe: luxury, exclusive, premium
- Best for: investor decks, luxury brands, VIP events
- Fonts: Cormorant Garamond + Montserrat
- Palette: pure black (#000), white (#fff), gold (#d4af37)
- Signature: black backgrounds, gold accents, minimal text, maximum impact
